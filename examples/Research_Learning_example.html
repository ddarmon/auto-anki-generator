How can a multiple integral over a region D be written as an iterated integral?
By choosing an order of variables and writing each variable's bounds as functions of the previously integrated variables, e.g. nested integrals of the form int_{a1}^{b1} int_{a2(x1)}^{b2(x1)} ... int_{ad(x1,...,x_{d-1})}^{bd(x1,...,x_{d-1})} f(x1,...,xd) dx_d ... dx_2 dx_1.
